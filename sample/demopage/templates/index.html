Virat Kohli

About Virat

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who plays Test and ODI cricket for the Indian national team. A former captain in all formats of the game, Kohli retired from the T20I format following India's win at the 2024 T20 World Cup.

Achievements

Kohli was a key member of the Indian team that won the 2011 Cricket World Cup, 2013 Champions Trophy and 2024 T20 World Cup and captained India to win the ICC Test mace three consecutive times in 2017, 2018, and 2019.

Centuries

As of 2023, he has scored 71 centuries in international cricket, making him one of the most dominant batsmen of his generation. Kohli has surpassed legends like Sachin Tendulkar and Brian Lara in terms of centuries scored, showcasing his exceptional batting skills and consistency.
